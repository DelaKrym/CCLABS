What we know so far about [ DEON ]? Deon is a creature that reacts to emotions and has two states of being; idle and active state. It feeds on green diatoms for growth. It changes its state after human interactions. It communicates with its neighbour by sending signals to them.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature It eats by the movement of food towards the central orb. It changes its motion and state with the mouse. When the mouse gets close to it, it changes color to send an emotional signal. It sends a signal by pressing the spacebar.
